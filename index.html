Who are we?

My Restaurant is the first stop off the street flavors we established under the name BigRestaurant. We produce fast foods,rice,mussels,pastas and much more,in accordance with the Albanian Food Codex and on a daily basis,and ship them to our branches.

In addition to the traditional taste, we bring a new breath to the street cuisine with our different recipes,always hot,fresh and specially cooked for whenever you want.
